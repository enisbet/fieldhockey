Graphs

This is a graph of each players total goals, separated by goals and shootout goals. This is created in Google Sheets and it updates automatically when I input player goals daily from each practice.

This is a column chart created in Excel used to display team stats by quarter. This is useful for the coaches to understand which quarters the players are performing better in.

This column chart is helpful when the coaches want single out players who need to perform better in a specfic stat. It is also helpful for the players to see what they need to work on.

This is an in-depth column chart. It is useful when the coaches and players want one graph to summarize multiple stats. It also allows players and coaches to compare stats with other players.

This Tableau line graph shows the team's goalies (Anna and Gillian) save %. A save % is (number of saves)/(number of goal attempts). This graph is given to the coaches and goalies so they understand what the goalies highest and lowest percents were throughout the season.

This is a graph that I currently update at every practice. A penalty corner (PC) is a penalty given against the defending team. Keeping track of penalty corners througout practice is important because they can result in the opposing team getting a goal. At a competition, the team will want to have the players who tend to give the least amount of PC's on the field.

This graph shows Sofi's progress throughout the offseason. It shows that her turnovers are increasing. This graph allows her to be more aware of her possession of the ball throughout a game and motivate her to play smarter.

This line graph for goals scored is beneficial to compare which player scores the most so the team can have their highest scorers on the field. I labeled the max number of each month so the graph is more clear.

I have been using random data to experiment with Tableau and create examples for the coaches. This diagram is helpful for the coaches to compare which player is the fastest and which week they exceled in. It is also helpful for the players to see their own accomplishments.
